Our Mission

GPAC is a premier partner in providing responsive, scalable, Supply Chain Management, Training and Logistics support solutions. Our mission is, to deliver quality, trust and satisfaction by removing limitations, elevating customer service, providing lasting personal services and solutions to our customers in an innovative and cost effective manner. GPAC, is a Service Disabled Veteran- Owned small business (SDVOSB) located in Fayetteville N.C, (fifteen minutes outside of Fort Bragg). GPAC consist of Six major core areas of expertise. (1.) Global Combat Support System(GCSS)- Army (inclusive of the following: Standard Army Retail Supply System(SARSS), Standard Army Maintenance System Enhanced (SAM-E), Property Book Unit Supply Enhance (PBUSE), additional GCSS support provided FSR’s and Training), (2.) New Equipment Fielding, (3.) Property Accountability, (4.) Inventory Management, (5.) Warehouse Management, (6.) Transportation/ Fleet Management. GPAC’s number one priority is to compliment any operation with real world, relevant experience that will execute the mission without failure. GPAC’s ultimate goal is to surpass our customer’s expectations by providing exceptional logistics services with Integrity, Efficiency, Accountability and Excellence which are our core values. Having been in deployed situations, as both soldiers and contactors, team GPAC fully understands the importance of supporting the U.S Armed Forces Logistics requirements.

Our Differentiator

GPAC understands the full spectrum of the U.S Armed Forces Enterprise Resource Plan (ERP), Systems Applications Products(SAP) and concept of Integrated solutions. GPAC, team is comprised of senior level, Quartermaster Corp military veterans who fully understand the needs and support required by our customers. GPAC has a cadre of experts highly trained and well educated in all aspects of logistics. Our workforce is 100% committed to becoming the premier logistics providers in our industry. GPAC is more than just an experienced contractor. We are a proven partner, working together with our customers to meet requirements and then reach beyond them, identifying underlying organizational goals and objectives, areas for continuous improvement, process streamlining, or increased quality. As a customer- focused, quality-driven organization, GPAC thrives on doing more, on exceeding customer expectations, and providing services that is superior to every other contractor in our industry. Our service is personalized and responsive. We do not employ a one-size- fits-all approach to requirements. While we draw upon our diverse knowledge base when approaching new requirements, we meet individually with every customer. We seek to understand their unique organizations and operations, the goals and objectives they have set for themselves and those to which they are held accountable, and the challenges to success. We incorporate that knowledge into our management approach, making our customers’ challenges our challenges, their goals our goals, and their successes our successes. To do so, we fully utilize and hold ourselves accountable. Our team of Logistics experts are all certified by the National Property Management Association (NPMA).
