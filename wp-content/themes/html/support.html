Core Support / Services

  • Global Combat Support Systems (GCSS)

    GPAC provides computer-based Training, Augmentee and FSR support services for the following Logistics automation systems, collectively called Standard Army Management Information Systems (STAMIS).
    • Global Combat Service Support Army (GCSS-A)
    • Standard Army Maintenance System-Enhanced (SAMS-E)
    • Property Book Unit Supply-Enhance (PBUSE)
    • Standard Army Retail Supply System (SARSS)
    • Unit Level Logistics Systems (ULLS)
    • Standard Army Maintenance System-Fleet Management (SAMS-FM)

  • Property Book Support

    GPAC provides support to organizations within the Department of Defense and other Government Agencies. Our elite logisticians can assist in maintaining property accountability and in conducting inventories. Our property book analysts have technical expertise on using the U.S. Army's Logistics automation systems, collectively called Standard Army Management Information Systems (STAMIS).
    • Global Combat Service Support Army (GCSS-A)
    • Property Book Unit Supply-Enhance (PBUSE)
    • Unit Level Logistics Systems (ULLS)

  • Warehouse Operations

    GPAC provides detailed stock control and warehousing augmentation in support of the following Classes of supply I, II, IIIP, IV, VII, and IX Supply Support Activities (SSAs) and other warehouse operations. Inventory and asset management is what we do.

    GPAC provides a support staff that is highly experienced in the areas of Transportation and Fleet Management, specifically in the arena of ground movements of personnel and equipment, civilian and military Fleet Management tracking systems, compliance in Transportation DOT regulations, warehousing of equipment, and all types of freight movement.

  • Staff Augmentation

    GPAC provides staff support and/or FSR for Logistics Operations from CORPS level to Company. Operations include New Equipment Fielding, New Equipment Fielding, Warehouse Operations, Logistics Analysis, Property Book, Transportation/ Fleet Management, and STAMIS Operations.
